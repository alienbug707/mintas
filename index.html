THE NUDIE COMMUNITY

The Nudie Community is an NFT collection of 10,000 unique ERC-721 tokens stored on the Ethereum Blockchain. Each will be computer generated from 300 + hand drawn traits consisting of various rarities and will be non-sequentially minted at one price for fair distribution.

Each NFT comes with exclusive rights and a membership to the Nudie Community, including functioning utilities and ever-growing club features. The possibilities are limitless, just like the everlasting life spans of these other worldly Nudies on the blockchain.

Check out our roadmap and utility for exciting plans for the future!

MEET THE NUDIES

Many millennia ago, 10,000 Nudies left their home planet of Nudetopia to form the ‘Nudie Community’. Their goal was to explore the galaxy and help evolve civilizations that they would find along the way.

So, naturally Earth was their first stop. The Nudies gave humans futuristic knowledge and a companion to evolve alongside them, known as a cat. In return, humans honored the Nudies with worship and art. They even built structures like the pyramids, which resembled their ship.

As light years passed, the Nudies began to wonder about Earth. Their curiosity led them to sneak covert agents among the feline companions they left behind that were more Nudie than a cat in the form of a ‘sphynx’.

These feline sphynx could transfer the collected data back to the Nudies via ultrasonic purrs. Much to their surprise, their influence on Earth surpassed all expectations. But so did the forces that the nudies did not approve of.

The humans consolidated their authority and power around centralized figures and places. Rather than then writing the humans off, they decided to set sail through the cosmos to visit Earth once more. They are ready to step onto Earth and help push society into its next level of advancements.

Their first step will be decentralization so that Web 3, Cryptocurrencies, NFT’s and DAO’s could soon bring back power to the people. It will be an exciting and challenging time, but their return would be a cosmic event like no other.

Why did you choose cats?
Not only does the founder have three Sphynx of her own, but cats have played a prominent role throughout history. The coexistence of cats and humans dates back to the earliest human settlements. The ancient Egyptians believed cats were magical creatures that would bring blessings for their owners.They viewed them as divine protectors and treated them with such respect that statues, jewelry, and artwork were made in their honor. Cats were so special that those who killed them, even by accident, were sentenced to death.
